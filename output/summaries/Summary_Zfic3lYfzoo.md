## Overview
This video from Tempo's "Bocor Alus Politik" explores how former President Jokowi has transformed his Solo residence into a tourist destination dubbed "Solo's Wailing Wall" (Tembok Ratapan Solo). Hosts Cica and Pram discuss their visits to Jokowi's home on Jalan Kutai Utara 1, revealing an orchestrated operation where the former president receives hundreds of daily visitors, provides free t-shirts, and maintains a structured system for managing tourists. The discussion reveals how Jokowi uses this setup for what they term "post-power therapy" while potentially maintaining political influence, though his power appears to be waning under the Prabowo administration.

## Key Highlights

The investigation reveals that Jokowi has effectively converted his former residence into a political tourism operation. Rather than living there, he uses the house as an office to receive visitors from 10 AM to 6 PM daily, while actually residing at another property called Geria Sedah Mirah, 4 kilometers away. The operation is highly organized with CCTV surveillance, security protocols managed by former presidential security officer Syarif, and a team of eight people orchestrating visitor flows. Visitors must surrender their belongings except phones, cannot take photos or videos themselves (staff do this), and receive branded t-shirts featuring Jokowi's image with words like "militant" and "religious." The hosts estimate this merchandise operation costs approximately 1.5 billion rupiah annually, with around 1,000 t-shirts printed weekly.

The tourism operation appears partially orchestrated through coordination with local government and travel associations offering "presidential packages" that bundle visits to Jokowi's residence with other Solo attractions. Former Jokowi staff member Bambang Sabtono plays a key role as an influencer, creating daily social media content from the residence and sometimes organizing visitor activities like chanting and group prayers for Jokowi's health. The setup drew attention when social media users created parody content comparing it to Jerusalem's Western Wall, leading to police questioning of content creators. Despite this organized effort to maintain relevance, both hosts note that Jokowi's political influence appears to be declining under Prabowo's administration, with limited access to the current president and reduced roles for Jokowi allies in government.

## Implications

This analysis suggests Jokowi is attempting to maintain political relevance through carefully managed public engagement, but his influence is diminishing as Prabowo asserts independent leadership. The tourism operation reveals both the former president's reluctance to fully retire from public life and the organized nature of his continued political activities, raising questions about the boundaries between personal activities and political maneuvering by former leaders.
